What "synthetic" means.

Every architectural layer started as a synthetic experiment. "What if we just did this by hand?" Every one that worked became the spec for what to build in code.

Synthetic is not fake. It's the proof that justifies the architecture. The gap between synthetic and architectural is the roadmap.

Where it breaks is where the roadmap begins. The breaks aren't failures — they're the spec for what the architecture needs to solve.
